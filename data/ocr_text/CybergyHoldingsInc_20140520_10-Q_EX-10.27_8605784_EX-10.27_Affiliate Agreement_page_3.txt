agreements to MA. MA shall promptly review such agreements and advise Company as to what

Schedule A to ensure that the agreements comply with requirements of local law in the Territory, and

liability under such local and federal laws of the U.S.

content and documentation, and Company's end user license agreement as it may be modified by
Company for use in the Territory. The relationship between the corporate user and Company and/or its
Affiliates shall be as specified in the applicable Company end user license agreement. Notwithstanding
the foregoing, as between Company and MA, MA shall be responsible as defined for providing
customer and technical support to end users in the Territory. MA will notify Company immediately in
the event that it is unable to respond effectively to any end users’ requests.
